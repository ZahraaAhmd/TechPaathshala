Founder’s success story

We, a group of four friends deeply passionate about design & coding despite weaker academic marks, came together to establish Techstalwarts, a technology company offering a range of services, from ui\ux to web development and platform development to cloud computing.
We have groomed and mentored numerous such passionate individuals who, despite having limited knowledge are now successfully leading major projects within our organization. We believe that passion should be the driving force behind pursuing a career or building business in IT, and academic performance should not be a hindrance.
This conviction led us to create a platform specifically for individuals who are passionate about tech but may not have excelled academically.
